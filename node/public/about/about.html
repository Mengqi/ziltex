Who are you?

    I am Mengqi Zong, currently working as a software engineer in San Francisco Bay Area. Before joining the industry, I studied at Columbia University in Machine Learning as a master student. I also worked on online gaming data compression algorithms using Artificial Intelligence for 9YOU when I was a junior in college. And I got paid for the new algorithm I designed and that was my first job. Because of my strong interest in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, I used to want to get myself a PhD in Machine Learning. But then I realized that PhD actually did not fit in my interest in the long run. But... I used to be a ShD.

What does ZILTEX stand for?

    It means "my plain-text website". I used to lurk on the Internet as "Zil", so "ZIL" stands for me. As to "TEX", it comes from the idea that most of the content in this site is in plain text.

Where did all this start?

    I was watching a techtalk of Linus Torvalds on git. And I found one of his comments on log system was quite inspiring (The quote above). Later on, having experienced some other things, I realized that how sad it was to barely remember anything about my past - neither the good ones nor the bad ones. So I made up my mind to set up my own "log system" to record what I have been thinking about. Also, it's interesting to learn how to set up a web site.
